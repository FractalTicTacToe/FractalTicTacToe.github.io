Fractal Tic-Tac-Toe

Website Created By: Jonathon Kuo

Rules

  • Players take turns, just like regular tic-tac-toe
  • Each play in a small board determines where the next player plays on the big board, where each spot on the small board corresponds to one of the small boards on the big board
  • The objective is to win the small boards like regular tic-tac-toe in a winning pattern on the big board
  • If it still doesn't make sense, just try it out and see how the game works!
